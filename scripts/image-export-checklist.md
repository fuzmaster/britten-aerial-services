# Image Export Checklist

- Export JPGs at 1800 to 2400px wide for hero and portfolio images.
- Use 80 to 85 quality for web JPGs.
- Avoid RAW, DNG, and full-size TIFF uploads.
- Use clean filenames like `bas-real-estate-drone-photography-peabody-ma-hero.jpg`.
- Keep video backgrounds short, compressed, muted, and under 15 seconds where possible.
