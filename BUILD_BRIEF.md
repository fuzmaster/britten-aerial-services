# Britten Aerial Services Website Build Brief

## Core goal

Rebuild Britten Aerial Services into a clean, professional, conversion-focused website that makes real estate agents, homeowners, property managers, contractors, and businesses trust the company quickly and request a shoot.

## Positioning

Position Britten Aerial Services as a Massachusetts real estate media and property documentation company first, with drone capability as the advantage.

Do not lead with generic scenic drone photography. Lead with property outcomes:

- Better listing media
- Aerial property context
- Roof and inspection documentation
- Fast digital delivery
- FAA-compliant drone operation

## Primary pages

1. Home
2. Services
3. Portfolio
4. About
5. FAQ
6. Contact / Book a Shoot

## Design rules

- Clean, premium, local service-business feel
- Big readable text
- Strong CTAs
- No cluttered sliders
- No dumping full galleries
- Mobile-first contact flow
- Use real work, not generic stock

## CTA language

Primary CTA: Book a Shoot
Secondary CTA: View Services
